# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, technical leads, founders, and collaborators reviewing Jing Kewei's frontend engineering profile. They need to understand capability, project depth, technical taste, and contact paths within a short scan.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio presents Jing Kewei as a frontend engineer who can build production systems, bridge frontend and backend work, integrate hardware and third-party services, and use AI-assisted development in real work. Success means the page feels credible, memorable, fast, and easy to contact from.

## Brand Personality

Technical, kinetic, precise. The experience should feel like an engineered spatial resume rather than a static document.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic resume templates, centered hero plus equal cards, purple-blue AI gradients, fake dashboard screenshots, overused Inter typography, dense tables, decorative status dots, and pages that read like a copied SaaS landing page.

## Design Principles

1. Show project depth through motion and structure, not through long paragraphs.
2. Preserve resume facts while making the hierarchy easier to scan.
3. Use WebGL as atmosphere and spatial context, not as decoration that hides content.
4. Let each major section use a distinct layout family.
5. Keep motion purposeful, responsive, and respectful of reduced motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for all text and controls. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, reduced motion, mobile-first layout collapse, readable Chinese text, and contact links with semantic mail and tel behavior.
